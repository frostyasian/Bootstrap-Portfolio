About Me

My name is Zuoyi and I love to rock climb. I found climbing on May 30th, 2018 from a friend who was a on and off climber. She showed me climbing and I immediately fell in love with the sport. It proved a challenge not only physically but also mentally. I like to see the routes as individual problems that can be solved in multiple ways. There is no right way or wrong way to climb a route but there are ways that are easier than others depending on your height, strength, and flexibility.



Climbing has become an outlet for me to relieve stress as well as have fun. It has made me venture into uncomfortable situations that I had to learn to overcome. I think climbing has taught me a lot of patience and if you put in the work, you will get results whether how small those results may be. I think thats why I'm so attached to climbing, that and all the friends and family I have created because of the wonderful community. I would reccomend anyone to try climbing becuase you wouldn't know if you like it until you are already hooked.
